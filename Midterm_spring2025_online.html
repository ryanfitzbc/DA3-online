For the following questions, use the data file: dataTickets1.csv (already uploaded to Jupyter Hub).

The data set contains information about drivers who were stopped for speeding in Massachusetts for 2 months in 2002. The file has the following information (columns):

  • Gender - the driver's gender (male or female)
  • Amount - amount of the speeding ticket (in US$)
  • MPHover - # of miles per hour over the speed limit
  • Age - the driver's age in years
  • OutTown - 1=driver is from out of town, 0=driver is from in town
  • OutState - 1=driver is from out of state, 0=driver is from in state
  • Police - whether the officer was from the state or local police
